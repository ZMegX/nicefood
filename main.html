Introduction

Nice is located in the South of France, in history the city belonged to Italy before France. It was a county of Italy, with its own language, the border between Nice and France was drawn by the Var a river that comes from the South Alpes. It has always been influenced by the French culture as also the election were written in French and Nissart (italian slang). Here on this webpage we are gonna focus on the food culture and make you discover the local food specialties that you can taste in Restaurants or kiosk around the city.
